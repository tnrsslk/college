About college


Astana IT University opens doors to early professional self-discovery, providing opportunities for acquiring a profession, employment, or pursuing further education at a university through a continuous education system. Upon completion of the college program, graduates receive a state-issued diploma, enabling them to continue their education at Astana IT University, other universities in Kazakhstan, or abroad in related fields. Our college not only equips students with practical skills but also fosters a foundation for lifelong learning and career advancement. Join Astana IT University for a transformative educational journey that sets the stage for a successful and fulfilling future.

Student life

Astana IT University actively fosters student self-governance, an integral component of student life that operates with consideration for the opinions of each student. The student self-governance system is an initiative-driven, independent, and communal form of student activity. Astana IT University hosts over 40 student clubs catering to various interests. These clubs provide students with opportunities for engagement, collaboration, and the pursuit of shared passions, enhancing the overall university experience.
